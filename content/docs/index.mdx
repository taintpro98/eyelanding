# Welcome to ALumiEye

ALumiEye is a trading intelligence platform built for Vietnamese stock market and crypto traders. It collects AI-generated trading signals, tracks your positions live, and gives you a clear view of the market — all in one place.

Whether you are just starting out or have been trading for years, ALumiEye is designed to be simple to use and powerful enough to make a real difference in your trading.

---

## What ALumiEye Does

ALumiEye connects to a smart signal engine (**eyebroker**) that monitors the market around the clock. When a trading opportunity is detected, it appears instantly on your dashboard as a **signal** — telling you the asset, whether to buy or sell, the price, and how strong the signal is.

Beyond signals, the platform helps you:

- **Track every open position** — see live profit/loss, stop loss distance, and position status at a glance
- **Understand the market** — top movers, price changes, and overall market sentiment
- **Analyse individual assets** — get a trend rating and short/mid-term direction for any symbol
- **Visualise your portfolio** — see how your capital is allocated across all holdings
- **Watch your favourite assets** — keep a personal list of symbols you care about

## Who Is ALumiEye For?

**Beginner traders** — The dashboard gives you a simple overview. Signals tell you what to consider buying or selling without needing to analyse charts yourself.

**Active traders** — Filter signals by symbol, side, and type. Monitor multiple positions in real time. Get alerted when market sentiment shifts.

**Experienced traders** — Use the Analysis page for deep per-symbol trend ratings. Review position capacity and stop loss distances to manage risk precisely.
