# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is ALumiEye?

ALumiEye is an AI-powered trading intelligence platform that helps traders detect alpha opportunities, track institutional money flow, and analyze markets with advanced machine learning. We combine real-time market data with proprietary AI models to surface high-probability trading signals.

### What makes ALumiEye different?

We focus on three pillars: real-time market intelligence, smart money tracking, and AI-powered signals. Our platform is designed for serious traders who want an edge—not just another charting tool.

### Is there a mobile app?

Our web app is fully responsive and works on mobile browsers. A native mobile app is planned for future release.

## AI & Signals

### How does the AI generate signals?

Our AI models analyze multiple data dimensions including order flow, price action, volume profiles, and alternative data sources. We use ensemble machine learning techniques trained on historical market data to identify patterns that precede significant price movements. Each signal includes a confidence score and risk metrics.

### How accurate are the signals?

Signal accuracy varies by market conditions and asset class. Our Pro plan signals have historically shown higher accuracy due to access to additional data and advanced models. Past performance does not guarantee future results.

### Can I use signals for automated trading?

API access is available on Institutional plans. Contact sales for programmatic access.

## Pricing & Plans

### Is there a free plan?

Yes! Our Starter plan is free forever and includes basic AI signals, a 5-asset watchlist, daily market briefs, and email support. It's a great way to explore the platform before upgrading to Pro.

### What's included in the Pro trial?

Pro includes a 14-day free trial with full access to all Pro features. No credit card required to start.

### Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. All plans are month-to-month. Cancel before your renewal date to avoid charges.

## Technical

### Which markets are supported?

We currently support US equities, major forex pairs, and popular cryptocurrencies. See [Supported Markets](/docs/supported-markets) for full details.

### Is my data secure?

Yes. We use bank-level encryption for all data in transit and at rest. We never store your trading credentials—we use read-only API connections where applicable.

### Do you offer an API?

API access is available on Institutional plans. Contact sales for details.
