# Getting Started

This guide will help you set up ALumiEye and start detecting alpha.

## Create Your Account

1. Click **Start Free** on the landing page
2. Enter your email and create a password
3. Verify your email address

## Configure Your Watchlist

Add assets you want to track:

- US equities
- Major forex pairs
- Popular cryptocurrencies

## Understanding AI Signals

Our AI analyzes multiple data dimensions to generate signals:

- Order flow
- Price action
- Volume profiles
- Alternative data sources

Each signal includes a confidence score and risk metrics.
