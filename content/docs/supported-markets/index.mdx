# Supported Markets

ALumiEye currently covers two markets: the **Vietnamese stock market** and **cryptocurrency**. Each market has its own set of assets monitored by the signal engine.

| Market | Plan required | Trading hours |
|---|---|---|
| [Vietnamese Stocks](./stocks) | Free | 9:00 – 14:30 GMT+7 (HOSE) |
| [Cryptocurrency](./crypto) | Pro / Premium | 24 hours / 7 days |

---

## Switching Between Markets

If you are subscribed to both Stocks and Crypto, use the **Market Toggle** in the top navigation bar to switch. All pages — Dashboard, Signals, Positions, Analysis, Portfolio — update to show data for the selected market.
