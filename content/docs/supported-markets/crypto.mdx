# Cryptocurrency

The Crypto market covers the most liquid cryptocurrency pairs, all quoted in USDT (Tether). Available on **Pro and Premium** plans.

---

## Covered Assets

| Symbol | Asset |
|---|---|
| **BTCUSDT** | Bitcoin |
| **ETHUSDT** | Ethereum |
| **BNBUSDT** | BNB (Binance Coin) |
| **SOLUSDT** | Solana |
| **XRPUSDT** | XRP (Ripple) |

Additional assets are added based on liquidity and signal quality. Check the Signals and Market pages for the current full list.

---

## Key Dashboard Metrics

When your selected market is Crypto, the Dashboard shows:

| Metric | What it means |
|---|---|
| **BTC Price** | Current Bitcoin price in USD |
| **ETH Price** | Current Ethereum price in USD |
| **Market Cap** | Total value of all crypto assets combined |
| **24h Volume** | Total trading volume across crypto markets in the last 24 hours |

---

## Trading Hours

Crypto markets are open **24 hours a day, 7 days a week**. Signals can appear at any time.

> **Tip:** Check the Signals page during high-volume periods such as the US market open (21:30 GMT+7) and Asian market hours for the most active signal windows.

---

## Crypto vs Stocks

| | Stocks | Crypto |
|---|---|---|
| **Trading hours** | Market hours (HOSE) | 24/7 |
| **Volatility** | Generally lower | Generally higher |
| **Signal frequency** | Tied to market sessions | Continuous |

Higher volatility in crypto means signals can move fast — always check the signal strength (Quantity) before acting.
