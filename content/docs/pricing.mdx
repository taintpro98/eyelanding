## Free Plan

**Price:** Free, forever

The Free plan is the best way to experience ALumiEye without any commitment. You get access to the Dashboard, Market overview, and a basic view of trading signals.

**Best for:** Traders who want to explore the platform and get a feel for AI-generated signals before committing.

**What you get:**
- Dashboard with live market KPIs
- Market page: top movers and sentiment
- Signals page: view the latest signals (filtering locked)
- Vietnamese stocks market included

**Limitation:** You cannot filter signals, track positions, or access analysis on the Free plan.

---

## Pro Plan

**Price:** $99 / month

The Pro plan unlocks the full trading workflow — signals with filtering, live position tracking, per-symbol analysis, and watchlist management.

**Best for:** Active traders who follow signals closely and manage multiple positions at once.

**What you get, on top of Free:**
- Filter signals by symbol, side, and type
- Positions page: track all open and closed trades with live P&L
- Analysis page: trend ratings (A–F), short and mid-term signals for any symbol
- Watchlist: follow your favourite assets
- Crypto market access (BTCUSDT, ETHUSDT, BNBUSDT, SOLUSDT, XRPUSDT…)
- Priority support

## Premium Plan

**Price:** Contact sales (custom)

The Premium plan is designed for professional traders, funds, and teams who need everything ALumiEye offers plus infrastructure access.

**Best for:** Professional traders and institutions managing significant capital.

**What you get, on top of Pro:**
- Portfolio visualisation: allocation chart with live returns, auto-refresh every 30 seconds
- AI Insights: market direction forecasts and sentiment analysis powered by advanced models
- API access: programmatic access to signals and market data
- Custom integrations: connect ALumiEye data to your own systems
- Dedicated account manager
- SLA guarantee

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time?

Yes. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

### What markets does each plan include?

The Free plan includes the Vietnamese stocks market. Pro and Premium also include crypto. Additional markets may be added in the future.

### How do I manage my subscription?

Go to **Billing** in the top-right account menu. From there you can see your current plan, expiry date, and upgrade options for each subscribed market.

### Is there a free trial for Pro?

Contact us through the app to ask about trial options.
