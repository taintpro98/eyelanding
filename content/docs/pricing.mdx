## FAQ

### Can I change plans?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

### Is there a free trial?

Pro plan includes a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start with Starter.

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards and wire transfer for Institutional plans.
