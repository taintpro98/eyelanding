# Features Overview

ALumiEye gives you six core tools, each designed to answer a specific trading question. This page summarises all of them. Click any feature to read the full guide.

---

## Dashboard

**"What is the market doing right now?"**

The Dashboard is your starting point. It shows the most important numbers for your selected market — index levels, foreign capital flow, and the 3 most recent signals — so you can get up to speed in seconds.

**Stocks market shows:** VN Index, HOSE Value, Net Foreign flow, VN30
**Crypto market shows:** BTC price, ETH price, market cap, 24h volume

---

## [Signals](./ai-signals)

**"Should I buy or sell right now?"**

The Signals page displays every buy and sell signal generated by the AI engine, in real time. Each signal tells you the asset, direction, strength, and price.

- Signals update continuously — no need to refresh
- Filter by symbol, side (buy/sell), or signal type
- Colour-coded strength indicator so you can prioritise the strongest signals

[Read the full Signals guide →](./ai-signals)

---

## [Positions](./smart-money)

**"How are my trades performing?"**

The Positions page shows every trade that has been opened, with live data including current price, profit/loss percentage, distance to stop loss, and position capacity.

- Filter by status: Running, Opening, Opened, Closing, Closed
- Refresh live price for any position with one click
- Click a position to see its full order history

[Read the full Positions guide →](./smart-money)

---

## [Market](./market-intelligence)

**"Which assets are moving today?"**

The Market page gives you a quick scan of the whole market. See the top gaining and losing assets, along with an overall sentiment reading.

- Top movers table with price and 24h change
- Market sentiment: Bullish / Neutral / Bearish with percentage breakdown

[Read the full Market guide →](./market-intelligence)

---

## [Analysis](./market-intelligence#analysis)

**"Is this asset trending up or down?"**

Search any symbol on the Analysis page to get a full trend breakdown:

- **Overall rating** from A (very bullish) to F (very bearish)
- **Mid-term trend** — direction, signal strength, and momentum over weeks
- **Short-term trend** — direction and signal for the current swing
- Recent signals and open positions for that symbol

[Read the full Analysis guide →](./market-intelligence#analysis)

---

## Portfolio

**"How is my capital spread across my trades?"**

The Portfolio page visualises all your holdings in an interactive allocation chart. Each slice shows the symbol, weight, entry price, current price, and return.

- Auto-refreshes every 30 seconds so your returns stay current
- Total portfolio value and cash balance displayed at the top
- Click any slice to go directly to that position's detail page

---

## Watchlist

**"Which assets do I want to follow?"**

Add any symbol to your Watchlist for quick access. The Watchlist shows the latest price for each asset and acts as a shortcut — one click takes you to its signals and analysis.

> Watchlist is available on **Pro and Premium** plans.
