# Features Overview

ALumiEye provides a comprehensive suite of tools designed for serious traders who demand an edge in the markets.

## Core Capabilities

### Real-time Market Intelligence

Stay ahead with live market data, order flow, and institutional activity across multiple asset classes. Our system aggregates data from exchanges, dark pools, and alternative sources to give you a complete picture.

### Smart Money Tracking

Follow the flow of institutional capital and identify where the smart money is moving before the crowd. Our algorithms detect unusual accumulation patterns and large block trades.

### AI Signal Detection

Machine learning models analyze patterns and generate actionable trading signals with quantified confidence. Each signal includes expected value, risk metrics, and timing recommendations.

### Portfolio Analytics

Comprehensive portfolio insights, risk metrics, and performance attribution to optimize your strategy. Track exposure across your entire portfolio in real time.

### Advanced Charting

Professional-grade charts with custom indicators, drawing tools, and seamless multi-timeframe analysis. Built for traders who need precision.

### Risk Insights

Real-time risk exposure, position sizing recommendations, and scenario analysis to protect your capital. Make informed decisions with confidence.
