# Smart Money Tracking

Institutional investors often move markets. ALumiEye helps you identify and follow their activity before it becomes obvious to the crowd.

## What is Smart Money?

Smart money refers to capital controlled by sophisticated investors—hedge funds, family offices, and institutional traders—who typically have more information, better analysis, and longer time horizons than retail traders.

## How We Track It

### Block Trade Detection

We monitor for large block trades that typically indicate institutional activity. These trades often appear in dark pools or as negotiated prints.

### Accumulation Patterns

Our algorithms detect when institutions are quietly accumulating positions over time. We look for:

- Volume spikes without price movement
- Unusual options activity
- Dark pool accumulation

### Distribution Warnings

Get notified when smart money appears to be distributing. Early detection can help you avoid holding through institutional sell-offs.

## Using Smart Money Data

### In Your Watchlist

Add assets to your watchlist and we'll track smart money flow for each. You'll see:

- Net flow direction (accumulation vs. distribution)
- Flow strength (how confident we are in the signal)
- Historical context (how current flow compares to typical)

### With AI Signals

Combine smart money tracking with our AI signals for higher conviction. When both align, the probability of a successful trade increases.

### Alerts

Set up alerts for:

- New accumulation in a watched asset
- Distribution warnings
- Unusual volume spikes
