# Real-time Market Intelligence

ALumiEye aggregates data from multiple sources to deliver a comprehensive view of market activity.

## Data Sources

Our platform ingests data from:

- **Exchanges** — Direct and consolidated tape data
- **Dark Pools** — Off-exchange activity
- **Alternative Data** — Order flow, options flow, and sentiment signals

## Features

### Live Order Flow

See real-time buy and sell pressure as it happens. Understand where large orders are being placed and how they impact price action.

### Volume Profile

Identify key price levels based on volume distribution. Our volume-at-price analysis helps you spot support and resistance zones before they're obvious.

### Institutional Activity

Detect when institutions are accumulating or distributing. Our algorithms flag unusual block trades and dark pool activity.

### Multi-Asset Coverage

Currently supported:

- US equities (NYSE, NASDAQ)
- Major forex pairs (EUR/USD, GBP/USD, USD/JPY, etc.)
- Popular cryptocurrencies (BTC, ETH, and major altcoins)

## Best Practices

1. **Set up alerts** — Get notified when unusual activity occurs in your watchlist
2. **Review pre-market** — Check overnight flow before the session opens
3. **Correlate with signals** — Combine market intelligence with AI signals for higher conviction
