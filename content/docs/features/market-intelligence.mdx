# Market & Analysis

ALumiEye gives you two complementary tools for understanding what the market is doing: the **Market page** for a broad view, and the **Analysis page** for a deep dive into a specific asset.

---

## Market Page

The Market page answers the question: **"Which assets are moving, and in which direction?"**

### Top Movers

A table of the most active assets in your current market, sorted by the size of their price change. For each asset you can see:

| Column | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Symbol** | Asset ticker |
| **Name** | Full asset name |
| **Price** | Current price |
| **24h Change** | Percentage change over the last 24 hours (green = up, red = down) |

> **How to use it:** The top movers table is a quick scanner. If you see a symbol you follow shooting up or down significantly, go to the Analysis page to understand whether the move is backed by a trend or just noise.

### Market Sentiment

Below the top movers, you will find the overall market sentiment — a measure of whether buyers or sellers are in control.

| Sentiment | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Bullish** | More buyers than sellers — the market is generally rising |
| **Neutral** | Balanced — no clear direction |
| **Bearish** | More sellers than buyers — the market is generally falling |

The sentiment is shown as three percentages that add up to 100%. For example: **60% Bullish / 25% Neutral / 15% Bearish** means the market is leaning heavily bullish.

> **For beginners:** In a bullish market, buy signals carry more weight. In a bearish market, sell signals are more reliable. Always check the sentiment before acting on a signal.

## Analysis Page

The Analysis page answers: **"Is this specific asset in a good position to trade right now?"**

### Searching for a Symbol

1. Type the asset ticker in the search box (e.g. `VNM`, `BTC`, `ETH`)
2. Click **Search** or press Enter
3. The page populates with everything ALumiEye knows about that asset

The URL updates to include the symbol so you can bookmark any analysis.

---

### Overall Trend Rating

At the top of the analysis, you get a single letter grade for the asset's current trend:

| Rating | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **A** | Very strong bullish trend — conditions are highly favourable for buys |
| **B+** | Good bullish trend — generally safe to lean long |
| **B** | Mild bullish trend — more caution needed |
| **C** | Neutral or mixed — no clear edge |
| **D** | Mild bearish trend — lean short or avoid longs |
| **F** | Strong bearish trend — conditions favour sellers |

> **Beginner tip:** Look for assets rated **B+ or A** when considering a buy trade. Avoid entering a new buy on an asset rated **D or F**.

---

### Mid-Term Trend

The mid-term trend reflects the asset's behaviour over the past several weeks — the "big picture."

Each trend card shows three values:

| Value | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Trend direction** | Up (↑), Down (↓), or Sideways (→) |
| **Signal** | Bullish, Bearish, or Neutral |
| **Strength** | Strong, Moderate, or Weak |

**Example:** Mid-term → Up / Bullish / Strong means the asset has been rising consistently with good momentum.

### Short-Term Trend

The short-term trend reflects the last few days of price behaviour. It uses the same three values.

**How to use both together:**

| Mid-term | Short-term | What to think |
|---|---|---|
| Bullish | Bullish | Strong alignment — high-confidence buy setup |
| Bullish | Bearish | Pullback in an uptrend — could be a buy dip or a reversal warning |
| Bearish | Bearish | Strong alignment — high-confidence sell setup |
| Bearish | Bullish | Bounce in a downtrend — treat with caution |

---

### Recent Signals for This Symbol

Below the trend cards, you see the last 4 signals for this asset (with pagination). This lets you see the pattern of recent AI activity — for example, whether Open signals have been followed by Increase signals, confirming momentum.

---

### Active Positions for This Symbol

At the bottom, any open positions for this asset are listed. Useful for checking whether a signal aligns with a trade you already have open.

> Active positions on the Analysis page are available on **Pro and Premium** plans.
