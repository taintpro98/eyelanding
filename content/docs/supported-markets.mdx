# Supported Markets

ALumiEye currently covers two markets: the **Vietnamese stock market** and **cryptocurrency**. Each market has its own set of assets monitored by the signal engine.

---

## Vietnamese Stock Market

The Stocks market focuses on publicly listed Vietnamese companies, tracked through the HOSE exchange.

### Key Indices Monitored

| Index | What it represents |
|---|---|
| **VN Index** | The main benchmark index of the Vietnamese stock market |
| **VN30** | The 30 largest and most liquid stocks on HOSE |
| **HOSE Value** | Total trading value on the HOSE exchange for the day |
| **Net Foreign** | Net buying or selling by foreign investors — a key sentiment indicator |

> **What is Net Foreign?** Foreign institutional investors are closely watched in the Vietnamese market. When Net Foreign is positive, foreign funds are net buyers — often considered a bullish signal. When negative, they are net sellers.

### How to Interpret VN Index

- **Rising VN Index** — The overall market is gaining. Conditions favour bullish signals.
- **Falling VN Index** — The overall market is losing ground. Signals on the sell side may be more reliable.
- **VN Index vs individual stock** — A stock can go up even when VN Index falls, and vice versa. Always check the individual stock's Analysis page rather than relying solely on the index.

### Vietnamese Stock Signals

Signals on the Stocks market are generated for individual Vietnamese company tickers. Examples include `VNM` (Vinamilk), `VIC` (Vingroup), `HPG` (Hoa Phat), and many others across all sectors.

## Cryptocurrency Market

The Crypto market covers the most liquid cryptocurrency pairs, all quoted in USDT (Tether).

### Covered Assets

| Symbol | Asset |
|---|---|
| **BTCUSDT** | Bitcoin |
| **ETHUSDT** | Ethereum |
| **BNBUSDT** | BNB (Binance Coin) |
| **SOLUSDT** | Solana |
| **XRPUSDT** | XRP (Ripple) |

Additional assets are added based on liquidity and signal quality. Check the Signals and Market pages for the current full list.

### Key Dashboard Metrics for Crypto

When your selected market is Crypto, the Dashboard shows:

| Metric | What it means |
|---|---|
| **BTC Price** | Current Bitcoin price in USD |
| **ETH Price** | Current Ethereum price in USD |
| **Market Cap** | Total value of all crypto assets combined |
| **24h Volume** | Total trading volume across crypto markets in the last 24 hours |

### Crypto vs Stocks

| | Stocks | Crypto |
|---|---|---|
| **Trading hours** | Market hours (HOSE: 9:00–14:30 GMT+7) | 24 hours / 7 days |
| **Volatility** | Generally lower | Generally higher |
| **Signal frequency** | Tied to market sessions | Continuous |

> **For beginners new to crypto:** Because crypto markets never close, signals can appear at any time. It is worth checking the Signals page at different times of day, especially during high-volume periods like US and Asian market overlaps.

---

## Switching Between Markets

If you are subscribed to both Stocks and Crypto, you can switch between them using the **Market Toggle** in the top navigation bar. All pages — Dashboard, Signals, Positions, Analysis, Portfolio — update to show data for the selected market.
